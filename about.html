Our Journey

Key Milestones

From regional beginnings to UEFA and FIFA membership

1946

Foundation

Kosovo Football Federation established as a regional entity within the Yugoslav football system, laying the groundwork for organized football in the region.

2008

Independence

The Republic of Kosovo declares independence, opening the door to international sporting recognition and a sovereign football identity.

2014

First FIFA Friendly

Kosovo plays its first FIFA-sanctioned international friendly against Haiti in Mitrovica — a historic 0–0 draw witnessed by 17,000 fans.

2016 ✦

FIFA & UEFA Membership

The landmark year. Kosovo becomes the 210th FIFA member (May 3) and 55th UEFA member (May 13), officially entering world football.

2018

Nations League Debut

An undefeated run — 4 wins, 2 draws — secures promotion from League D to League C in the inaugural UEFA Nations League campaign.

2020

EURO Playoff Run

The Dardanët reach the EURO 2020 qualifying playoffs, defeating North Macedonia before a narrow semi-final exit in Skopje.

2024+

The Next Chapter

With a talented generation led by Muriqi, Rashica, and Zhegrova, Kosovo targets its first major tournament qualification.
