Our Journey

Key milestones in Kosovo football history

1946

Foundation of Kosovo Football

Kosovo Football Federation established as a regional branch within the Yugoslav football system.

2008

Kosovo Declares Independence

The Republic of Kosovo declares independence, paving the way for international sporting recognition.

2014

First FIFA-Recognized Friendly

Kosovo plays its first FIFA-sanctioned friendly match against Haiti, ending in a 0–0 draw.

2016

FIFA & UEFA Membership

Kosovo is officially accepted as the 210th member of FIFA and 55th member of UEFA on May 3 and May 13.

2018

UEFA Nations League Debut

Kosovo competes in the inaugural UEFA Nations League, winning their group undefeated and earning promotion.

2020

EURO 2020 Playoffs

The Dardanët reach the EURO 2020 qualifying playoffs, a major milestone for the young footballing nation.

2024

Continued Growth

Kosovo continues to develop its football infrastructure and national team, competing in UEFA Euro and World Cup qualifiers.
